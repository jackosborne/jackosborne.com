Edinburgh, Scotland. January 2020

Jack Osborne is a Digital Product Designer based in Brooklyn, New York, via Greenock, Scotland.

For the past three years, I served as a Senior Design Manager at Foursquare, where I helped lead, mentor, and grow the design team. Before that, I worked on Foursquare's 0→1 team, Labs, where I developed software that showcased how location could enhance a user's experience, making their everyday lives easier. Before joining the Labs team, I spent six years working on Foursquare's consumer applications, City Guide and Swarm.

At the beginning of my career, I spent five years at some of Scotland's most respected creative agencies. My work included a variety of clients, from national charities to some of the country's largest property and job markets. During this time, I also spoke at events, contributed to various publications, and had a project featured in the national press.

Outside of work, I often find myself getting lost in cooking channels on YouTube, buying overpriced vinyl records from my youth, seeking out dogs willing to give me a bit of attention, and consistently telling myself that this will be my last dollar slice of pizza.
